About me

Driven, Dedicated, Eager to Learn and Grow!

I am a Software Engineer with professional experience in full stack application development and embedded systems. I have hands on experience developing reliable systems in constrained environments for medical devices, including developing, debugging, and testing embedded software mainly in C/C++. In addition, my background includes designing and implementing responsive and user focused mobile and back end solutions with a focus in Java, C#, and native Android applications. I graduated from Rutgers University School of Engineering with degrees in Computer Engineering and Computer Science, and enjoy bridging the gap between low level systems and high level applications, ensuring performance, stability, and maintainability at every layer.

On the embedded side, my work has encompassed a number of different micocontollers (STM32, ARM, AVR), communication protocols (UART, SPI, I2C), and hardware peripherals (motor encoder, hall effect sensor, EEPROM, etc.) in both Interrupt driven and RTOS based systems. I have built device drivers and custom communication protocols and have a proven ability to implement new features and debug complex hardware and software issues to deliver efficient, production ready firmware in fast paced environments.

My full stack experience includes developing multiple native applications, following clean architecture principles, integrating RESTful APIs, database management and integration (MySQL, MS SQL Server, SQLite), designing and implementing unit tests (JUnit), incorporating feedback to improve user experiences, managing CI/CD pipelines, and implementing secure authentication. My experience also includes utilizing Spring Boot and .NET frameworks in addition to working with Docker containerization.

I have worked in Linux environments writing scripts in Bash and Python and worked with a number of AI tools. My passion projects have including a concussion detecting football helmet and a RAG LLM Document Analyzer. I enjoy working across the full software development lifecycle in agile environments where ideas turn into production ready applications, and have excellent communication skills which enables me to be an effective collaborator with both technical and non-technical teams.

I have training in IEC 60601-1, IEC 62304, and ISO 14971 with experience developing software in compliance with FDA 510(k) submissions and EU MDR regulations across the full SDLC with risk management, including verification and validation tests and procedures. I also have training and experience being HIPAA compliant while working with medical data.
